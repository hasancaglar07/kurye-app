2.2 Paket ve takip verileri

  • Kullanıcının eklediği takip numarası, takip bağlantısı veya takip metni.

  • Kesin konum gerektiğinde ayrı ve bağlama uygun sistem izni istenir; izin reddi temel manuel takip işlevini engellemez.

2.4 Bildirim verileri

  • Bildirim izni durumu.
  • Push token ve cihaz platformu.
  • Kullanıcının hangi paketlerde bildirim istediği.
  • Bildirimin teslim/etkileşim durumu.

2.5 Abonelik ve satın alma verileri

  • App Store product ID, entitlement, satın alma/yenileme/iptal durumu, deneme durumu ve anonim RevenueCat app user ID.
  • Kurye tam kart numarası, CVV veya Apple ID şifresini görmez ve saklamaz. Ödeme Apple tarafından işlenir.

2.6 Cihaz, güvenlik ve ürün analitiği

  • Uygulama sürümü, işletim sistemi, cihaz sınıfı, locale, ülke, network durumu ve hata/performans bilgisi.

  • Güvenlik, kötüye kullanım ve servis bütünlüğü için IP adresi, istek zamanı ve teknik loglar.

Analytics event’lerine takip numarası, adres, e-posta, paket metni veya serbest kullanıcı içeriği yazılmaz.

2.7 Destek iletişimi

  • Kullanıcının destek talebinde ilettiği iletişim bilgisi, mesaj, ek ve sorun çözme geçmişi.

2. Data categories we collect

Account and authentication

  • Email address and optional display name.
  • Provider user identifier and permitted profile fields from Sign in with Apple or Google.
  • Session and security identifiers.

An account is not required to see the first package result. It is requested when the user needs sync, purchases, export, or cross-device persistence.

Package and tracking data

  • Tracking number, tracking link, or tracking text supplied by the user.
  • Optional package title or note.
  • Carrier identifier and carrier events.
  • Status, timestamp, general location, ETA range, delay risk, and confidence.
  • Notification and handoff preferences.

Users should not enter addresses, government identifiers, payment-card data, or sensitive information that is not required for package tracking.

Location

  • Kurye does not collect precise location by default.
  • Approximate location or delivery region may be used only when the user enables an arrival feature.
  • A contextual system permission is requested when required. Denial does not block manual tracking.

Notifications

  • Permission state, push token, platform, package notification preferences, and delivery/interaction status.

Purchases

  • App Store product ID, entitlement, purchase/renewal/cancellation status, trial state, and RevenueCat app user ID.
  • Kurye does not receive or store a full card number, CVV, or Apple ID password. Apple processes payment.

Device, security, and analytics

  • App version, OS, device class, locale, country, network state, crash and performance data.
  • With consent, PII-free activation, onboarding, paywall, and feature events.
  • IP address, request time, and limited technical logs for security and abuse prevention.

Tracking numbers, addresses, email, package text, and free-form content are excluded from analytics properties.

Support

  • Contact information, messages, attachments, and troubleshooting history voluntarily provided to support.

3. Sources

We collect data from the user, permitted Apple/Google authentication, carriers or merchant integrations, Apple/RevenueCat purchase events, app technical telemetry, consented analytics, and support interactions.

The native app does not use advertising cookies. Connected web pages may use essential security/session cookies and consent-based analytics.

4. Purposes and legal bases

We process data to provide tracking, accounts, synchronization, notifications, AI-assisted parsing and estimates, purchases, security, support, analytics, and legal compliance. Depending on context, the legal basis is performance of a contract, consent, legitimate interests, or a legal obligation. Consent may be withdrawn prospectively at any time.

5. AI and automated processing

  • The mobile app never contains Google/Vertex credentials or the central gateway secret.
  • A trusted Kurye backend applies authentication, quota, entitlement, ownership, and retention controls before calling the central AI gateway.
  • The server selects the versioned operation and prompt. Users cannot select a Google model or system instruction.
  • Responses are schema-validated and fail closed to needs_review or blocked when uncertain or invalid.
  • ETA and delay predictions are decision-support estimates, not carrier records or delivery guarantees.
  • Kurye does not use AI output to make legal or similarly significant automated decisions about a user.

6. Service providers and disclosures

Data may be disclosed, only as necessary, to Apple, Google, RevenueCat, the central AI gateway, carriers and merchant integrations, Sentry or a similar error provider, a consented analytics provider, hosting/database/security vendors, authorities, and professional advisers. Contractual and security safeguards require appropriate or equivalent protection.

7. Sale, sharing, and ATT

  • Kurye does not sell personal information for money.
  • The default release does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies for targeted advertising.
  • Kurye does not “sell or share” data for cross-context behavioral advertising as those terms are used by the CCPA.
  • If this changes, privacy labels will be updated and ATT permission will be obtained before tracking begins.
  • Refusing ATT never gates core functionality, price, or rewards.

Prepared ATT purpose string:

``text If you allow it, we use a device identifier to measure campaign performance and improve acquisition relevance. Declining does not affect Kurye features. ``

Do not show the ATT prompt when the current release does not perform cross-app tracking.

8. Retention and deletion

  • Guest history remains on the device until local data is cleared or the app is removed.
  • Account-based structured package history remains until deleted by the user or the account is closed.
  • Raw AI input is retained only for the shortest period required to complete and safely retry a request and is not used as persistent model-training data.
  • Security logs, support records, and purchase/accounting records are retained only for operational or legally required periods.
  • Active-system deletion begins without undue delay; encrypted backup rotation may take up to 30 days.

Production retention settings must match this written policy before release.

9. Security

Kurye uses TLS, server-only secrets, access control, least privilege, rate limiting, signed/TTL uploads, PII-free analytics, prompt-injection and unsafe-output validation, and backend entitlement verification. No system is completely secure; legally required breach notifications will be made.
