2. Kurye 1.0’ın veri yaklaşımı

Kurye 1.0 hesapsız çalışır. Ad, e-posta, parola, rehber, kesin/yaklaşık konum, fotoğraf, ses veya ödeme kartı istemez. Reklam göstermez ve uygulamalar arası takip yapmaz.

2.1 Yalnız cihazda tutulan bilgiler

  • Kullanıcının eklediği takip bağlantısı, numarası veya taşıyıcı durum metni.
  • Paket başlığı, oluşturulan özet, timeline ve teslimat tercihleri.
  • Uygulama dili, bildirim tercihi ve yerel kullanım geçmişi.

Bu bilgiler cihazdaki uygulama alanında tutulur. Ayarlar’daki yerel veri silme işlemi veya uygulamanın kaldırılması bu yerel geçmişi kaldırır. Kurye 1.0 cihazlar arası hesap senkronizasyonu yapmaz.

2.2 İstek sırasında geçici işlenen bilgiler

Kullanıcı “Taşıyıcıyı Bul” dediğinde ilgili takip metni, yalnız o isteği yanıtlamak için TLS üzerinden Kurye backend’ine ve merkezi AI gateway’e gönderilir. İstek gövdesi ve AI yanıtı Kurye backend’i veya gateway tarafından kalıcı olarak kaydedilmez; erişim loglarında takip içeriği yer almaz. Bu içerik reklam, pazarlama, profil oluşturma veya model eğitimi amacıyla kullanılmaz.

Kullanıcı takip için gerekmeyen adres, kimlik numarası, ödeme bilgisi veya hassas kişisel veri girmemelidir. AI sonucu bir tahmindir; taşıyıcının resmî kaydı ve teslimat garantisi değildir.

2.3 Abonelik ve satın alma bilgileri

Apple ve RevenueCat şu bilgileri işler: anonim RevenueCat App User ID, ürün kimliği, entitlement, deneme, satın alma, yenileme, iptal, faturalama sorunu, geri ödeme ve restore durumu. Kurye kart numarası, CVV veya Apple ID parolasını görmez.

Kurye backend’i ücretli erişimi doğrulamak için yalnız RevenueCat App User ID ve entitlement/satın alma durumunun sınırlı bir kopyasını tutar. Bu bilgiler reklam takibi için kullanılmaz.

2.4 Destek

Kullanıcı e-posta veya telefonla destek isterse, gönüllü olarak paylaştığı iletişim ve mesaj içeriği talebi çözmek ve yasal yükümlülükleri yerine getirmek için gerekli süreyle tutulabilir. Destek mesajında takip numarası, parola, ödeme kartı veya kimlik belgesi gönderilmemelidir.

1. Controller

This policy applies to the Kurye iOS app, production backend, support channel, and Kurye legal web pages.

2. Kurye 1.0 data approach

Kurye 1.0 works without an account. It does not request a name, email, password, contacts, precise or coarse location, photos, audio, or payment-card information. It shows no advertising and performs no cross-app tracking.

2.1 Information kept only on the device

  • A tracking link, number, or carrier status text added by the user.
  • Package title, generated summary, timeline, and delivery preferences.
  • App language, notification preference, and local usage history.

This information stays in the app’s local device storage. The local-data deletion control in Settings, or uninstalling the app, removes that history. Kurye 1.0 does not provide account-based cross-device sync.

2.2 Information processed transiently for a request

When the user selects “Detect Carrier,” the relevant tracking text is sent over TLS to the trusted Kurye backend and central AI gateway only to answer that request. The request body and AI response are not persistently stored by the Kurye backend or gateway, and tracking content is excluded from access logs. The content is not used for advertising, marketing, profiling, or model training.

Users should not enter an address, government identifier, payment information, or sensitive personal data that is unnecessary for tracking. AI output is an estimate, not an official carrier record or delivery guarantee.

2.3 Subscription and purchase information

Apple and RevenueCat process an anonymous RevenueCat App User ID, product identifier, entitlement, trial, purchase, renewal, cancellation, billing issue, refund, and restore state. Kurye never receives a card number, CVV, or Apple ID password.

The Kurye backend keeps only a limited copy of the RevenueCat App User ID and entitlement/purchase state to verify paid access. It is not used for advertising tracking.

2.4 Support

If a user contacts support by email or phone, voluntarily supplied contact and message content may be retained only as needed to resolve the request and meet legal obligations. Users should not send a tracking number, password, payment card, or identity document in a support message.

3. Purposes and legal bases

| Processing | Purpose | Legal basis | | --- | --- | --- | | Transient tracking-text processing | Parse carrier text and return the requested result | Contract performance / user request | | RevenueCat ID and purchase state | Purchase, restore, and paid-access verification | Contract performance, legal obligation | | Security and rate limiting | Service integrity and abuse prevention | Legitimate interests | | Support communication | Respond to the request | Contract performance / legitimate interests |

4. Service providers and transfers

  • Apple: App Store distribution, In-App Purchase, and notification permissions.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription, entitlement, restore, and webhook synchronization.
  • Central AI gateway and Google Vertex AI: Server-to-server, single-request AI processing. Credentials and the gateway secret are never included in the mobile app.
  • Hosting, network, and security providers: Infrastructure required to operate the HTTPS service.

Providers may process data in the United Kingdom, European Economic Area, United States, or other countries. Adequacy decisions, standard contractual clauses, or equivalent safeguards are used where applicable.

5. Analytics, crash reporting, and tracking

  • No remote product-analytics destination is enabled in the Kurye 1.0 production configuration.
  • The Kurye 1.0 binary does not include Sentry or another remote crash/performance SDK.
  • ATT is not used; no advertising identifier is accessed; there is no third-party advertising or data-broker sharing.
  • App Store privacy answers must be updated before this behavior changes.

6. Retention and security

  • Tracking requests and AI responses are not retained in Kurye systems after the real-time request completes.
  • The RevenueCat App User ID and entitlement record remain only as needed to verify purchase status; RevenueCat event IDs are retained to prevent duplicate processing.
  • Support and legally required purchase records are kept only for necessary operational or legal periods.
  • Controls include TLS, server-side secrets, least privilege, rate limiting, webhook authorization, schema validation, and log redaction.

7. Rights and requests

Applicable GDPR/UK GDPR, Turkish data-protection, and CCPA/CPRA law may provide rights of access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, objection, and withdrawal of consent. Kurye does not sell personal information or share it for cross-context advertising.

Request: coinfeedback@icloud.com with “Kurye Privacy Request” in the subject. Do not include unnecessary tracking, payment, or identity data. Local package history can be deleted directly in Settings. Apple subscriptions are managed separately in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

8. Children and changes

Kurye is not directed to children under 13. A higher local digital-consent age applies where required. Material policy changes will be communicated in the app or through another appropriate channel, and the effective date will be updated.
