Before contacting support

  • For a missing update, compare the latest event with the carrier's official page and retry after checking your connection.
  • For notifications, confirm iOS notification permission and that alerts are enabled for the package.
  • For an existing subscription, use Restore Purchases while signed in to the same Apple ID.
  • For cancellation, open iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. Kurye cannot cancel an Apple subscription on your behalf.
  • For an App Store refund request, use Apple's Report a Problem service.

Privacy or account request

Email coinfeedback@icloud.com with “Kurye Privacy Request” or “Kurye Account Deletion” in the subject. Include the app name, the email used for the account, and the action requested. Do not send a tracking number, password, payment-card data, or identity document unless support specifically and lawfully requires it.
